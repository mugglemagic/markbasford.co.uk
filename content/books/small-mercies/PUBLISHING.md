## Publishing Paths

### Recommended: Digital First + Print
Launch on Leanpub (digital) and Amazon KDP (Kindle + paperback) simultaneously.

**Leanpub** (leanpub.com)
- Best for: technical non-fiction, developer/designer audience
- Royalties: 80% on sales above $7.99
- Format: Digital only (PDF, EPUB, MOBI)
- Control: You set the price, keep all rights, can update anytime
- Audience: Already tech/design readers
- Setup: Free, upload manuscript and go

**Amazon KDP** (kdp.amazon.com)
- Best for: Kindle ebook + print-on-demand paperback
- Royalties: 70% on ebooks under $9.99 (35% above), ~60% minus print costs on paperback
- Distribution: Amazon's global store
- Expanded distribution option makes paperback available to other retailers
- Setup: Free, straightforward dashboard

**IngramSpark** (ingramspark.com) — optional, for wider print reach
- Best for: Getting into bookshops, libraries, non-Amazon retailers
- Distribution: Global catalogue that bookshops and libraries order from
- Setup: Free (was $49, dropped in 2023). $25 fee for file changes after 60 days
- Can run alongside KDP — use KDP for Amazon, IngramSpark for everywhere else

### Alternative: Niche Publisher
If you want a publisher's name on the spine and professional production:

**Rosenfeld Media** (rosenfeldmedia.com)
- Publishes UX/design practitioner books
- You'd submit: proposal, chapter outline, sample chapter (all done)
- They handle: editing, design, production, distribution, marketing
- Royalties: Typically 10–15% of list price
- Timeline: 12–18 months from acceptance to publication

**Smashing Magazine** (smashingmagazine.com/printed-books/)
- Also publishes design/frontend books
- Similar submission process to Rosenfeld

### Not Recommended Right Now
- **A Book Apart**: Closed in 2024
- **Traditional mainstream publishing** (Penguin, etc.): The book's niche is probably too specific, and the timeline (agent search + publisher submission + production) could be 2+ years

## Pricing Guidance
- Leanpub: Suggested minimum $19.99, recommended $29.99 (readers can pay more)
- Kindle: $9.99 is the sweet spot for 70% royalty
- Paperback: £16.99–£24.99 depending on page count and production cost
- You can price differently on each platform

---

## Key Decisions to Make
1. **Digital only, or digital + print?** (Recommend both)
2. **Self-publish, or pitch to a niche publisher?** (Recommend self-publish first, publisher later if it does well)
3. **Cover designer** — worth paying for. Budget £300–£800 for a professional non-fiction cover
4. **ISBN** — needed for print. Free from Nielsen in the UK via their ISBN agency, or purchase a block
5. **Editing** — consider a professional copy-edit before publishing (~£0.01–0.02/word for the full manuscript = ~£600–£1,200)
